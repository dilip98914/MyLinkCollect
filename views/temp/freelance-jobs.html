Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find projects / Jobs ?

As a freelancers you just need to login and apply to the job against any jobs /project and directly connect with client. You can either send a message through internal chat or send an email and you can also directly call the client, if the client has given access to his/her phone number.

How will I get the payment from Client ?

Freelancers can have an agreement with the client and can work at his /her own terms & conditions as we are connecting freelancers directly with client.

What type of jobs I can do freelance?

In freelance, you can get any sort of jobs that are web-based and physical presence in the premise is not necessary. For example, you can get freelancing projects in India for designing, web development, creative, writing, editing, proofreading, mobile app development, digital marketing and others.

Where can I find freelancing jobs?.

There are so many websites where freelancing work in India has been posted. OnBench Mark is one such reliable name in this industry where you can apply for freelance projects from all over the world. You just need to view, shortlist and apply for the projects to start working.

Is it mandatory to work at an hourly rate?

No, it’s not mandatory to work only at hourly rate. You can discuss with the client about fixed rate or monthly rate also. It entirely depends on your preference and mutual agreement between freelancer & client.

Are these projects only for freelancers or for bench employees also?

No, it’s not only for freelancers. Client select an option for only freelancers but if they have chosen an option of “Contract” that mean they are also open for bench resources

Can I work from home in the freelance job?.

Yes, you can work from home in a freelancing job. It is the biggest advantage of self-employed freelance workers. However, many jobs required the presence of the location especially when they are hiring the bench.

Do I need any other skills apart from my domain for freelancing?.

Freelancing does not need any other skills apart from your domain expertise. You need to know every trending thing about your skillset and stay updated with the changing phase of technology. Though many freelance websites need to excel in bidding skills, on our website; you don’t need to do that.
