Frequently asked questions

How can I post the jobs for free?

You can use the free account of OnBenchMark and post a job for free of cost. In your free account, you can view 25 freelancer profiles, post jobs and deploy your bench.

How do I know the approval status of my job?

After posting a job on our board, it will take generally minimum time to get the approval. Until it gets the approval, the status will be showing In Review. After it gets the approval, the status will be changed to “Published” and the candidates can start applying for it.

Is it mandatory to work at an hourly rate?

No, it's not mandatory to work only at hourly rate. You can discuss with the client about fixed rate or monthly rate also.It entirely depends on your preference and agreement between freelancer & client

When do I need to pay for the posted job?

You can register and post jobs on OnBenchMark for free. However, to access the email id and phone number of the applicants you can opt the essentials and premium plans. We also offer custom plans to companies as per their requirements.

Why OnBenchMark is better than all the job posting platforms?.

OnBenchMark is a trusted platform both for freelancers and companies. Here you can get experienced and beginner freelance talents and contract employees from verified companies of almost all domain. You don't need to wait for long after posting the job as it will publish almost instantly and you will start receiving applications

What to do if I am looking for a job instead of posting one?.

If you are a freelancer or job seeker, you will have thousands of freelance and contract jobs posted in our jobs section
